Audio problems

If you are having audio problems on a Samsung device, please watch the tutorial below.

Video Transcript

  1. Open the settings app and go to accessibility settings
  2. Go to TalkBack
  3. Go to settings
  4. Go to text-to-speech
  5. Go to preffered engine
  6. Select the second option and then go back
  7. Tap the settings button next to preffered engine
  8. Go to install voice data
  9. Go to arabic
  10. Tap the download icon and wait
  11. Finally, choose a voice for the website (III recommended)

Even if you are on a different type of android phone, I reccomend following these steps (which may be slightly different) and changing the arabic voice to voice III as it is the best for the website.
